What is Envirogram?

Envirogram is a Social Media designed to benefit the environment. Simply upload pictures of positive acts toward the environment onto your feed. You can earn virtual currency called enviroicoins and comepete for spots on our global leaderboard.

How does it work?

There are many ways to earn envirocoins. Actions like planting a tree or recycling can earn you envirocoins. These envirocoins can be redeemed for prizes such as amazon gift cards.

About Us

Envirogram was founded by 4 SBHS Students. Arjun Khatri, Abhishek Yadav, Manav Patel, and Mukund Chanchlani. Our mission is to get millenials involved helping the environment.

Frequently asked questions

This side bar contains critical information in the form of FAQ'S that people have questioned before if you have any questions please go over these first before you visit the "contact me" section and send an email

Is there a limit to the amount of submissions per day?

No, you may submit as many as you like.

Is there any other way to earn envirocoins?

Tweeting or posting on instagram about the app using the hashtag #envirogram can earn you 10 envirocoins.

Is my personal information safe?

Absolutely, we never share your personal information.

Can I donate money to Envirogram?

Yes, Donating will earn you envirocoins at 1$ per coin.
